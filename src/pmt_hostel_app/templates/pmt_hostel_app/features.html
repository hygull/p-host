Reading hall

A hall where number of students can stay and study silently. It is far from noice as well as the good thing about this hall is its silence and beauty.
Students won't face any kind of problem while studying.

Dining hall

A TV facilitaed hall with 4 dinings where approximately 35-40 students can sit while having breakfast, lunch, dinner etc. This hall is used for Hostel meeting as well as to celebrate other different kind of functions and festivals.

Playground

This small but nice playground is under the hostel campus which can be used to play games like football, Volleyball, Badminton, Cricket etc. PMT organizes price based games on special occassions like 15 August, 26 January, Annual day etc.
